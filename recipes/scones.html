Scones

This is a very simple recipe for scones. You can customize them by adding dried fruit or nuts. Half and half can also be substituted for milk if you wish.

These are most definitely the easiest and quickest things to make! I find it great if I have friends from mothers' group coming over and I only have literally minutes to make something before they arrive. With only 3 ingredients it's minimal fuss.

Ingredients:
  • 3 1/2 cups self-raising flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 cup cream

Steps:
  1. Pre-heat oven to 200C.
  2. Put all ingredients into a bowl. Mix roughly with a knife, but don't over mix.
  3. Dust bench or board with flour.
  4. Pat dough on bench until approximately 3-5cm high.
  5. Use an upside-down glass dusted with flour to cut out scones. Place scones on an oven tray.
  6. Bake for 20 minutes.
